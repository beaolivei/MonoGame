MonoGame: Quick and Angry

During the 2 following days, 2 events will take place: Quick and Angry & The Starving Games. Quick and Angry is about a car race where your goal will be to collect items on your way to the finish line. Careful, you have to pick all the required items before reaching the finish line to win the race!
The last man standing for this event wins: 500K Monodollars + Surprise Price.
